Green Power Club

Hi, We're the Green Power Club!

Nice to meet you.

Green Power Club is a group of students who got together to make a difference for the betterment of the environment. We create projects that help the environment or generate power in an environmentally-friendly way. We meet every Monday in room 163, and every Thursday in Conference Room 1.

If you'd like to see what we're working on now, check out our blog. If you want to see what we've done in the past, take a look at Past Projects. If you're a club member and want to get on the forum, try Forum.
