Hi, We're the Green Power Club!

Nice to meet you.

Green Power Club is a group of students who got together to make a difference for the betterment of the environment. We create projects that help the environment or generate power in an environmentally-friendly way. Every Monday, we meet at 2:30 in room 163 and every Thursday, we meet at 2:30 in Conference Room 1. The Monday meeting ususally ends around 3:30 and the Thursday meeting usually ends around 2:50, unless there is an impending deadline.

Try mousing over the green box at the side of the screen that says "NAVIGATION." Our progress on the current project is under "Blog," our previous projects are under "Projects," and our club forum is under "Forum."
